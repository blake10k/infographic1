Global Researcher Distribution

The global research community is concentrated in key regions, with Asia leading in sheer volume of researchers. This highlights the region's significant investment in R&D and higher education.

U.S. Faculty by Rank

The U.S. full-time faculty is distributed across various ranks. Full Professors make up the largest single group, followed by Assistant and Associate Professors, indicating a mature academic workforce.

U.S. Postgraduate Enrollment Trends

Enrollment in U.S. postgraduate programs has shown steady growth. While Master's programs enroll a significantly larger number of students, both Master's and PhD tracks have expanded over the last decade.

Profile of U.S. PhD Students

The U.S. PhD student body is diverse. A significant portion of students are international, highlighting the global appeal of U.S. research institutions. The gender balance is nearly even, though this varies by field.

Faculty Gender by Rank

A closer look at faculty demographics reveals a gap in gender representation at senior levels. While parity is approached at the Assistant Professor level, the proportion of women decreases at higher ranks.

Future Outlook: PhD Enrollment

Projections indicate a continued, moderate growth in PhD enrollment. This trend is driven by increasing demand for high-level expertise in technology, health, and data-driven fields.

Postgraduate Enrollment by Field

STEM fields dominate postgraduate enrollment, reflecting strong demand and funding. Business and Health Sciences also represent major segments, followed by a combined group for social sciences and humanities.
